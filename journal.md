# Protocol for Active Operations

## Phase 1: Recon (The "Cage")
Before we engage, we define the battlefield.
1.  **Identify Market Condition:**
    *   **Trending?** (Higher Highs/Lows) -> **Strategy: Trend Continuation (Pullbacks/Flags)**
    *   **Ranging?** (Bouncing between levels) -> **Strategy: Mean Reversion (Buy Support/Sell Resistance)**
    *   **Choppy/Undefined?** -> **Strategy: STAND DOWN.** (Cash is a position).

## Phase 2: Weapon Selection
Once the condition is identified, we pull the right tool:
*   **Trend Tool:** Fibonacci Retracements, Moving Averages (EMA 20/50), Bull/Bear Flags.
*   **Range Tool:** Horizontal S/R Levels, RSI Divergence, Bollinger Bands.

## Phase 3: The Scan
We don't stare at one chart hoping it moves. We scan multiple assets for **obvious setups**.
*   We look for the "easy" trades. If we have to squint to see the pattern, it's not there.
*   **Confluence is Key:** A Flag pattern *on* a Support level *with* Volume is a Tier 1 setup.

## Phase 4: Execution Criteria
Our "Cage" for pulling the trigger. We must check these boxes:
1.  **Structure:** Is the pattern clear?
2.  **Confluence:** Do at least 2 indicators agree? (e.g., Support + RSI Oversold)
3.  **Risk/Reward:** Is the potential profit at least 2x the risk? (2R minimum).
4.  **Bitcoin Check:** Is BTC doing something that will wreck this trade?

This approach gives us the freedom to hunt any setup, but the discipline to only strike when the odds are stacked in our favor.
